17.18 Double Teaming Artillery
When weather restricts the movement of Artillery Batteries, they were on occassion double teamed. If the Commander is successful in finding the extra horses, then the effects of the bad weather are ignored.

Double teaming attempts may be made once per battery, per day of battle (or game). Once the Commander fails to double team a battery, no more attempts may be made for any other batteries under his command.

To test for Double Teaming per battery, roll 2D10, and apply the modifiers. A score of 11+ indicates success, 10 or less indicates failure.
