Extra Movement
During tactical movement, the player may attempt to spend a CommanderAction to urge his formed troops forward faster.

Select which units this applies to, and select the extent of the extra move - 50m, 100m, 150m. (1", 2", 3")

The Extra Movement table shows the probability of the unit becoming disordered from this extra movement, as a function of the extra distance itself and the type of terrain they are moving through.

Roll 1D10 <= the resulting number to become disordered due to the extra move.
