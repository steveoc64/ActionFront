GT Movement Chart
The GT Movement Chart displays movement rates (in inches), for each type of ME, in each of the various deployment states.

- GT Movement is affected by weather, and terrain effects.

- GT Movement is not affected by unit formation.

Note that 10 "inches" equates to 1 whole grid square. The computer will apply new movement rates to all units each turn, and calculate the current grid square that the unit should be placed in at the end of each movement phase.
