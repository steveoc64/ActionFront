Entering Structures
To ENTER a structure, roll 2D10 and apply the modifiers. Cross reference the die roll with the Street Fighting class of the unit attempting to enter the structure.

If the Score is greater than or equal to the value of 'Ordered', then the unit occupies the structure in good order, setting up good fields of fire.

If the Score is less than the value of 'Ordered', then the unit occupies the structure in disorder, without good fields of fire.

Exiting Structures
To EXIT a structure, roll 2D10 and apply the modifiers. Cross reference the die roll with the Street Fighting class of the unit attempting to enter the structure.

If the Score is greater than or equal to the value of 'Exit', then the unit exits the structure, and is considered disordered.

If the Score is greater than or equal to the value of 'Ordered', then the unit exits the structure, and is considered ordered.

If the Score is less than the value of 'Exit', then the unit remains in the strucutre, and may try again to exit next turn.
