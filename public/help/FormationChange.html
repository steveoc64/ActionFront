Changing Formation
During tactical movement, a unit may change formation as part of its movement, which in turn affects the distance that the unit may move.

To use this table, cross reference the Movement Class of the unit (Either Divisional, or Ancien Regime), with the current formation. The resultant values show the movement cost of changing formation.

The resulting number is the number of inches that the unit may continue to move. If the value is Zero or negative, then the following notes apply :

  • 0 - Not Allowed
  • -1 - Formation change takes entire turn, unit may fire.
  • -2 - Formation change takes entire turn, no fire.
  • -3 - Formation change takes entire turn, no fire and disordered.
