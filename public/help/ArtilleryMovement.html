14.29.1 Artillery Movement
This chart shows the inches that a battery may move during the tactical movement phase.

Cross reference the Artillery Class, the weight of guns, and the type of movement required to read the inches available for movement.

Types of movement are defined as :

  • Regular - Regular movement whilst limbered
  • Gallop - Fast movement whilst limbered, at the risk of Horse Loss
  • Prolong - Hand wheel the guns whilst unlimbered
Foot Artillery
Foot Artillery may perform 2 actions per tactical turn from the following list :
  • Limber
  • Unlimber
  • Fire

Foot Artillery must remain within 1 Grid Square (direct or diagonal) of Formed Units of the same ME.

Horse Artillery
Horse Artillery may perform 3 actions per tactical turn from the following list :
  • Limber
  • Unlimber
  • Fire

Horse Artillery must remain within 2 Grid Squares (direct) or 1 Grid Diagonal of Formed Units of the same ME.
